Help and Support

1. Home Page

Welcome to the home page of **OnoAir**! From here, you can navigate to various sections of the website:

  • Manage Flights: Admin users can manage available flights.
  • Manage Destinations: Admin users can add and manage flight destinations.
  • Search Flights: Users can search for available flights based on origin and destination.
  • Book a Flight: Users can book available flights.
  • My Bookings: Users can view their past and upcoming flight bookings.
  • Help and Support: You are here now! This section provides assistance.

2. Booking a Flight

To book a flight, follow these steps:

  • Go to the **"Book a Flight"** page from the navigation menu.
  • Fill in your details, including your name, passport number, and flight number.
  • Click the **"Submit"** button to confirm your booking.
  • You will receive a confirmation message with your booking details.
  • Your booking will be saved and you can view it later in the **"My Bookings"** section.

3. Viewing Your Bookings

To view your booking history:

  • Go to the **"My Bookings"** page from the navigation menu.
  • You will see a list of your past and upcoming flight bookings.
  • Bookings are saved in your browser and you can check them anytime while browsing the website.

4. Searching for Flights

To search for available flights, do the following:

  • Go to the **"Search Flights"** page from the navigation menu.
  • Select your **origin** and **destination** from the dropdown lists.
  • Click the **"Search"** button to view all available flights matching your criteria.
  • The list of available flights will be displayed below the search form.

5. Admin Features

If you are an **Admin**, you have access to manage flights and destinations.

  • **Manage Flights**: Add new flights to the system, including flight number, origin, destination, and date.
  • **Manage Destinations**: Add new destinations that users can select when booking flights.

To add new flights or destinations, go to the respective pages in the Admin section and fill out the required fields. Once you add a flight or destination, it will be saved for users to see and book.
